Imagine Dragons

American indie rock band formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, USA. Became famous after the release of their debut studio album Night Visions in September 2012. The American magazine Billboard named them the brightest new stars of 2013. Participant: Dan Reynolds, Ben McKee, Wayne Sermon, Dan Platzman.
